The 100 Most Common Chinese Family Names

 

The list of Chinese family names or surnames is much shorter than in other cultures. For example, the total number of surnames (of native Chinese people) in China is just over 4,000. In the US, there are well over 150,000 surnames that are held by more than 100 people. In total, the US has over 6 million surnames. Consider yourself lucky that you are learning Chinese!

Learning the common Chinese surnames can be very helpful when you are reading texts. If you see a group of 2 or 3 characters and the first character is one of these surnames, even if you don’t know the other characters, you can recognize that it is a person’s name and continue reading without wasting time to look up the meaning. Learning these surname characters will also help you to remember the names of Chinese people you meet, as the name will be more familiar to you. Even learning just the top 20 will be very useful.
